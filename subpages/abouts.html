About Us!

Giftquipo

Welcome! We are glad that you want to learn more about who we are. Giftquipo is an new and upcoming company that is all about the people. We run because of you and in return we take care of you. We all love receiving gifts but when it is time to gift, we tend to think what do people gift to senior citizens, adults, kids, and babies. We answer those questions by providing our users gift ideas by simulating christmas game for each category.

The heartbeats of the website:

Shrey Patel, Tirth Patel, and Ria Chittargi

We are three extremely passionate website designers who pride in achieving great customer service and customer satisfaction. Our inspiration comes from “Always keep in mind the old retail adage: Customers remember the service a lot longer than they remember the price.” Lauren Freedman, Real estate agent.

We know how sometimes thinking of gift ideas gets annoying because we just can't think of what to gift. So, we decided to design this website to help our customers out by easing the pain and stress when looking for gift ideas during the crunch time.
